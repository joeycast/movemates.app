Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to Movemates. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Movemates, you may provide us with:

  • Profile information (name, date of birth, gender, profile photos)
  • Running information (pace, weekly mileage, commitment level, goals)
  • Location information (city, state)
  • Interests and bio
  • Messages and communications with other users
  • Move (group run) details you create or join

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use the app:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent in app)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, app crashes)
  • Location data (when you grant permission)

1.3 Third-Party Services

If you connect third-party services (such as Strava), we may receive information from those services in accordance with their privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Movemates service
  • Connect you with other runners based on your preferences
  • Enable messaging and group run coordination
  • Personalize your experience and improve our services
  • Send you notifications about moves, messages, and app updates
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Process premium subscriptions and payments
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (name, photos, running stats, bio, interests) is visible to other Movemates users based on your privacy settings. Your location is shared at the city/state level only.

3.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our app (hosting, analytics, payment processing, customer support).

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Movemates, our users, or others.

3.4 Business Transfers

If Movemates is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information at any time
  • Control your privacy settings (profile visibility, gender filters)
  • Block other users
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Request a copy of your data

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Free users have access to 30 days of chat history; Premium users have unlimited chat history. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Children's Privacy

Movemates is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information.

8. International Users

Movemates is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@movemates.app
